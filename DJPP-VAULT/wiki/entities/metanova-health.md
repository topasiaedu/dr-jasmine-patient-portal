# Entity — Metanova Health Sdn Bhd

**Kind:** Organisation (client / practice vehicle)  
**Sources:** [[wiki/sources/01-project-overview]]

## Role in the product

Metanova Health Sdn Bhd is the company behind **Dr. Jasmine’s private practice** and the **Dr. Jasmine Patient Portal**. The practice focuses on **metabolic health** and **diabetes reversal** through personalised dietary and lifestyle interventions.

## Clinical positioning (high level)

- Private GP / metabolic health orientation.
- Protocol palette includes LCHF, Mediterranean, intermittent fasting, low GI, and other evidence-based approaches — personalised per patient (per `docs/01-project-overview.md`).
